How to Play Super Tic Tac Toe

1. The Goal: Win 3 small boards in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win the game.

2. The Rule: You don't get to pick which board you play on!

  • If you place your mark in the Top-Right square of a small board...
  • ...your opponent MUST play in the Top-Right small board of the big grid.

3. Free Move: If your opponent sends you to a board that is already full or won, you can play anywhere you like (indicated by green highlights).
